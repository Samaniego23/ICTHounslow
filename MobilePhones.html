*Lost/Stolen Phone
Reporting lost or stoled equipment imply the following: 1.- Report it to Police as soon as possible. A copy of the report is needed to report it to ICT. 2.- Contact the Digital & IT Service Desk calling 0208 583 3838 or via Intranet>ICT Self Service Portal, create a ticket providing: asset number, phone number or IMEI number 3.- Inform you manager of the incident 4.- Request replacement in needed>
