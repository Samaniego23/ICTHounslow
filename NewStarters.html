Important Information...

Setting UP My New Device
Now that you have picked up your new equipment the next step is to complete the set up with your Microsoft account. As there are three types of devices follow this links to useful videos for the type of the device that has been allocated to you. It could be one of these: Lenovo Autopilot device, Microsoft Surface Pro and Lenovo ThinkPad Laptop
Where Can I Find Information
There is lots of useful information in the Hounslow Intranet. Open any web browser > type “Hounslow intranet” in the address book> press enter> Navigate to the subject of your interest.
About My Training/Learning Pool
Your training Pool account should be requested by your manager, an account will be created, and you will receive and email with your credentials and instructions how to access it. If you need to enquire about the status of this request call 3838 quoting you request reference number of the request (get it from your manager). .
What is ICT GetITDone Portal?
One of the main ways to get IT support is GetITDone Portal which can be reached from Hounslow Intranet, type Hounslow Intranet in the web browser address box and press enter > Navigate to Self Service Portal VIDEO. Another way to get help is calling 02085833838.
What About the Apps I need for my Work?
If your role implies the use of specific software, you need it to be installed on your new device It has to be requested by your Line Manager. If you need to inquire about the status of such request, you can call 020 8583 3838 quoting the reference number. Bear in mind that Microsoft Applications comes installed on your device as standard.
